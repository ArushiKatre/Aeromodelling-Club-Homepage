AEROMODELLING CLUB

NIT KURUKSHETRA
Welcome to the Aeromodelling Club of NIT Kurukshetra, where we bring together aviation enthusiasts who share a passion for building and flying model aircraft. Our club is dedicated to promoting the hobby of aeromodelling and providing opportunities for members to learn, build, and fly their models. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or just starting out, we welcome you to join us and discover the joy of aeromodelling!
Our club specializes in primarily 3 types of flying models which are RC Planes, Drones and Hovercrafts. Click the links below and check them out now!
